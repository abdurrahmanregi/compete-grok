## BLP Demand Estimation: Step-by-Step Guide

**BLP** stands for Berry, Levinsohn, and Pakes (1995), a seminal random coefficients logit model for estimating demand in **differentiated product markets** (e.g., automobiles, cereals). It solves key logit limitations: (1) IIA assumption (rigid substitution), (2) price endogeneity, and (3) unobserved heterogeneity. Estimation uses **GMM** with a **nonlinear inversion**.
